Benvenuto a Laugiano


This is a restaurant providing an exceptional blend of spirited cooking and gracious hospitality to our neighbors and friends.

br When the Italians sit down at an osteria they expect a quick, honest, and simple meal made from local produce served alongside a carafe of good wine. It’s the same at Laugiano, our cosy and warm osteria in the middle of old Copenhagen. Menus are short, food is served on platters and in small bowls for sharing, and you can order wine or beer ad libitum without any need for worrying about the check.
